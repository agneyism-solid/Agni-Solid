another blog post

2019-07-08T23:04:53+0400
I’ve got a lot to try to justify. There are some arguments going on which I haven’t yet contextualized nearly as well as I would like. I feel that there is a lot of innocent life on Earth that unfortunately too much effort is being made to antagonize unrighteously.

Of course there are degrees and levels of justifiability even in the mix of argument and fighting, but there really is a great deal of life that just plain deserves better safety and better sanity. I’m trying to find the right starting place, and slowly, on my regular site, I’m making progress. However, there is some work I can be doing with the blog type of format, that I think will help, even if it might not make sense to all audiences yet.


There is a vocal fight that has been waging for as many years as I’ve been aware of invisible voices, and apparently much of it is eternal. Much of the visible world denies this fight, and I fear that denial makes us weaker. However, at our present level of life, we’re just about doing all we can to survive even the conflicts that we can see, what to speak of mysteries less visible and sane.

I don’t think that the best thing to do is to draw unnecessary attention to it, so I’ll try not to be doing that. But I also don’t think denial helps. I’ve learned to speak of it and refer to it enough that I’m not that afraid of it any more, but a lot of people probably still are.

For me, the sensible approach toward saving life and sanity from all this is to give a very careful examination of what theism is, since I’m very sure that higher levels of life with their greater strengths are helping us much better than we know how to help our selves and each other. I don’t so much mean to debate varieties of theism or insist that theism in general is right for all. I just mean to acknowledge the possibility that stronger life does exist and the question of how cooperativity would happen between us and them.
